# WorkflowPilot — Business Brief
**Tagline:** Your first AI operations assistant.

---

## 1. Your Idea
- **Product:** Free AI chatbot that discovers workflow pain points and matches the right tool, agent, or automation.
- **What makes it different:** It doesn’t just answer questions. It records your specific pain, matches it to a curated catalog, logs gaps for R&D, and builds new solutions from real user demand.
- **Why you?** We own the data layer between pain and solution. Every conversation makes the system smarter.

---

## 2. Your Name + Market
- **Name:** WorkflowPilot
- **Market:** Solopreneurs and 1–10 person SMEs buried in admin, follow-ups, invoicing, scheduling, or compliance work.
- **One customer profile:**
  - **Age:** 25–45
  - **Pain:** Repetitive workflows eating billable hours; tried Zapier but it’s too technical; tried ChatGPT but it doesn’t remember their business
  - **Care about:** Getting time back, looking professional, not hiring a $4k/mo VA

---

## 3. Your Marketing
- **How they find us:** Meet them where they’re already complaining — Reddit, Telegram/WhatsApp groups, Instagram/TikTok DMs.
- **Where we sell:** Inside the chat. Free discovery → recommendation → guided setup → managed hosting.
- **Pick one channel, master it:** Telegram/Discord bot first. Lowest friction for this audience.

---

## 4. Your Tool Stack
- **Entry:** Chatbot in Telegram/Discord + web widget on landing pages
- **Discovery:** Pain extraction + catalog match engine
- **Delivery:** Self-serve guides → $197 guided setup → $399/mo managed hosting
- **R&D:** Weekly scored backlog turns unresolved pain points into new products

---

## 5. Your Pricing
- **Free:** Pain discovery + tool recommendations
- **$9.99/mo:** Unlimited recommendations + workflow library
- **$197 one-time:** Guided setup + custom implementation plan
- **$399/mo:** Managed agent hosting + Slack/WhatsApp line
- **Pricing philosophy:** Anchor high so $9.99 looks like a steal. Price on value, not cost.

---

## 6. Your Profit
- **PROFIT = INCOME − EXPENSES**
- **Revenue streams:**
  - Affiliate commissions: ~$8–25/conversion, ~80% margin
  - Digital products: ~$33.50 AOV, ~97% margin
  - Managed hosting: ~$399/mo, ~85% margin
- **Key metric:** Time saved per user. If we don’t measurably save time in the first session, we have no product.

---

## Quick Pitch
> WorkflowPilot is the free AI assistant that turns “I’m swamped with admin” into a specific tool, workflow, or agent. You chat, it matches, and if you want help implementing — we build and run it for you.
