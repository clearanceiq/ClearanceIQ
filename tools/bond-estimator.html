How bonds work

  • Single transaction bond: usually 1–3% of the duties, taxes, and penalties CBP could assess.
  • Continuous bond: covers all entries for 12 months; minimums are often $25,000–$50,000.
  • Your surety may require personal credit or company financials. Strong import history lowers the rate.
