# Bot Name Options

Selected: **KasiSetelbot** (@KasiSetelbot)

Other candidates considered:
1. Opsi (@opsibot) - professional, short
2. Kawal (@kawalbot) - BM for "control/manage"
3. Mbah (@mbahbot) - Javanese "wise elder"
4. Tuah (@tuahbot) - Hang Tuah reference
5. Jom (@jombot) - "come on" in BM
6. Lepak (@lepakbot) - youth culture "hang out"
7. Gile (@gilebot) - slang "crazy/powerful"
8. Klik (@klikbot) - "click", action-oriented
9. Ruang (@ruangbot) - "space/room", privacy vibe
10. Bicara (@bicarabot) - "speak/discuss", MY+ID shared

Why KasiSetelbot won:
- Indonesian slang energy: "kasi" (give) + "setel" (set/gear up)
- Sounds like "let's get it ready"
- 10 characters, memorable, has "bot" suffix for clarity
- Youthful, playful, local
