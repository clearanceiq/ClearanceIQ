BOT NAME OPTIONS
================

2. privagent (9 chars)

3. taskmate (8 chars)

7. smartbuddy (10 chars)

8. taskhive (8 chars)

9. opsscout (8 chars)

10. workmate (8 chars)
    Why: Simple collateral. Feels like an office companion, not a distant SaaS bot. Easy to recommend verbally.
