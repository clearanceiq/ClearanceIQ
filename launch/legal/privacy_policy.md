# Privacy Policy
**ClearanceIQ** — Last updated: 22 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ClearanceIQ ("we," "us," or "the Service") collects, stores, and protects your information when you use our Telegram-based AI assistant. We've tried to keep this plain, honest, and short — because we actually read these and you deserve better than wall-to-wall legalese.

---

## 1. What We Collect

To keep the agent running for you, we store the following:

- **Telegram User ID** — so the agent knows who you are across sessions.
- **Persona Configuration** — your saved preferences, settings, and any custom instructions you set up.
- **Conversation Logs** — the messages exchanged between you and the agent, which the agent references to give you contextual, useful responses over time.

That's it. We don't pull contacts, photos, geolocation, or anything beyond what the agent needs to work.

---

## 2. How We Store It

All data is kept in **encrypted JSON files stored locally on our servers** (not in a cloud SaaS database). Encryption is applied at rest. Only our operator has direct access to the underlying host environment, and access is restricted to a small set of credentials used solely for maintenance and security.

- **Storage format:** encrypted JSON
- **Access model:** operator-only, limited credentials

---

## 3. How Long We Keep It

We retain your data for **90 days** following your last interaction with the Service. After 90 days of inactivity, your conversation logs and associated data are automatically and permanently deleted.

---

## 4. Your Rights — Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your data at any time. You do not need to file a formal request.

**Just send the command `/delete`** from your Telegram conversation. This immediately removes all stored data linked to your account.

Additional user rights depending on your jurisdiction:

| Jurisdiction | What applies to you |
|---|---|
| Indonesia (PDP Law No. 27 of 2022) | Right to access, correct, and request deletion of personal data. |
| European Economic Area (GDPR) | Right to access, rectify, restrict processing, portability, and erasure. Contact us to exercise these rights. |
| California (CCPA/CPRA) | Right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of sale. We do not sell personal data. |

---

## 5. Sharing Your Data

**We do not share, rent, or sell your data to third parties.**

We may disclose information only if required by a valid legal process (e.g., a court order in Indonesia or applicable jurisdiction), or to enforce our Terms of Service against serious violations (e.g., fraud or illegal activity).

---

## 6. Data Location

Your data is stored on servers operated by the **Service owner in Indonesia**.

If you are in the EU or elsewhere, please note that by using the Service, your data is transferred to and processed in Indonesia. While Indonesia has been granted an adequacy decision by certain authorities for specific data frameworks, we treat Indonesia as the primary host country for all stored data.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

This Service is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the Service, please contact us and we will delete the related data.
