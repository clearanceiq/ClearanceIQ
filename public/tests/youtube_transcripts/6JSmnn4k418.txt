Welcome to our guide on navigating the USA Customs Clearance HS and HTS Lookup Tool. This resource is designed to help you narrow down your imports correct HTS code via a partial code or specific product description. Let's begin. On the Lookup Tool homepage, you can search for HTS codes and rate of duty using a product description or by entering the first four to six digits of the code, if you know it. With the first option, start by entering a product description and the tool searches through thousands of codes. For this example, we searched golf shoes. The search results you see are a list of possible HS codes matching the description. Review the results and descriptions carefully until you see the one that best matches your intended import. Now, you'll be able to see the products heading and subheading, description, unit of quantity if applicable, and specified rates of duty. We highly recommend that all importers then confirm the complete HTS code and corresponding duty rate with a licensed customs broker before purchasing for import. And that concludes our walk-through of the HS and HTS Lookup Tool. Understanding these codes and their impact on tariffs is vital for anyone involved in international trade.
