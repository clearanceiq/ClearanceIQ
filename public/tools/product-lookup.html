Paste a shopping or product page link — Amazon, Alibaba, 1688, Shopee, supplier site — and we’ll extract the product profile and map it to an HTS estimate with paperwork needs.

Product profile

How it works

  • Paste a product/shopping page URL.
  • We extract likely product description and category signals from the URL path and host.
  • Then we suggest a best-match HTS code, indicative duty rate, AD/CVD risk, and likely required paperwork.
  • We don’t parse proprietary pages or private data. This is planning reference, not a customs ruling.
