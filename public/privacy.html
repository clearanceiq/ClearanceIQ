Privacy Policy (PDPA-aligned)

Last updated: 2026-06-28. We may update this notice; continued use after changes means you accept the updated terms.

1. What we collect

Site usage data: anonymized pageviews, tool opens, submission events, timestamps, and session IDs generated in your browser. We do not collect name, email, or company details unless you submit them via a form.

2. Why we collect it

To operate and improve our customs tools, monitor performance, fix bugs, and understand which tools are most useful.

3. Cookies & local storage

We use essential cookies for site operation and localStorage to remember your PDPA consent choice and session ID.

4. Chat widget

The chat widget records messages you send for compliance assistance. Messages are stored to provide follow-up context. Do not submit sensitive personal data through chat.

5. Data retention

Telemetry events are kept in Cloudflare KV indefinitely unless you request deletion. Chat messages are retained for 90 days.

6. Your rights

You can opt out of telemetry by declining the banner on first visit. You can request deletion by emailing support@clearance-iq.com.
