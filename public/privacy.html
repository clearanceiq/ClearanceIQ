Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

What we collect

We collect minimal usage data to keep the service reliable and secure. That may include page paths, tool selection, and anonymized telemetry events tied to a local session token.

Cookies and local storage

We use browser storage only to remember your PDPA consent, theme preference, and usage context. We do not store payment-card data on our servers for free-tier usage.

API keys

If you sign up, we issue a per-account API key. Keep it secret. Treat it like a password.

Data retention

Telemetry is retained for operational analytics and model-training feedback only. You can request deletion by emailing clearanceiq@proton.me.

Third-party services

We may rely on hosting, analytics, and email providers. Their own privacy policies govern the data they process.

Contact

Questions: clearanceiq@proton.me or contact page.
