About ClearanceIQ

A free tool that helps US importers and Amazon FBA sellers avoid surprise duties, holds, and compliance gaps — before they pay a factory.

Why we exist

Most small importers learn their real landed cost after the container is already on the water. A wrong HTS code, an unflagged AD/CVD case, or a missing compliance doc can turn a profitable order into a loss. ClearanceIQ gives you a fast, plain-language read on duty, risk, and paperwork — free, no login required for a basic check.

Who runs it

ClearanceIQ is operated by an independent import-compliance team focused on making broker-grade screening accessible to solo importers and small sellers. We are not a customs broker and do not file entries — we help you prepare and plan.

Need a ruling or binding advice? We always point you to a licensed broker or CBP. Our output is a planning reference, not a legal or customs ruling.

How the data works (methodology)

  • HTS codes & duty rates — drawn from publicly available US HTS schedules and general duty columns. Special provisions (FTAs, Section 301, AD/CVD) are flagged where identifiable, but coverage varies by product.
  • Bond estimates — calculated from CBP continuous/single-transaction bond minimums; final premium depends on surety underwriting.
  • AD/CVD watch — indicative flags based on recorded cases; not a live exclusion list. Always confirm at trade.gov.
  • Update cadence — schedules change. Treat any figure as a snapshot, not a guarantee.

Every result on this site carries the line: "Planning reference only. Not a ruling. Verify with CBP or a licensed broker." That is not boilerplate — it is the honest limit of what a free tool can do.

Our data promise

  • Aggregated, anonymized usage trends may inform product improvement. We never expose your suppliers or volumes to other users.
