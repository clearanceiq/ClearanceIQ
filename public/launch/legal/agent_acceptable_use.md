# Agent Rules — A Quick Look
**ClearanceIQ** | Shown during onboarding

Welcome aboard. Here's the honest version of how things work around here.

---

## 🧭 Our Ground Rules

1. **Know what works — and what doesn't.**
   The agent knows a lot, but it doesn't know everything. Always double-check important facts (medical, legal, financial). If something sounds off, it probably is. We're here to save you time, not replace your judgment.

2. **Play it straight.**
   Don't ask the agent to generate illegal, deceptive, or harmful content. Don't try to jailbreak or manipulate it to bypass safety rules. If you're unsure whether a request is okay, just ask before you proceed.

3. **The agent may refuse — no hard feelings.**
   Some requests get a polite "no" because they're unsafe, illegal, or off-limits. The agent won't lecture you or report you. It'll just say it can't help and move on. No judgment.

4. **Your data stays yours — and ours to protect.**
   Everything you share is stored encrypted and privately. We don't sell, share, or leak your data. All stored data auto-deletes after 90 days of inactivity, and you can wipe everything anytime with `/delete`. That's a promise.

5. **Use it like a real human: keep it kind.**
   Harassment, abuse, and personal attacks have no place here. Treat the agent with basic decency — same as you would anyone else. It'll return the favor.

6. **Only you can use your account.**
   Don't share your Telegram session or agent credentials with anyone else. Access is tied to your Telegram ID, and letting others in may result in loss of access.

---

## 💬 A Few Works Best Practices

- Be as specific as possible for better results.
- If the answer feels off, push back and ask the agent to think again.
- If something's broken or confusing, just say so — feedback is always welcome.
- Need your data gone? Type `/delete` and it's gone.
