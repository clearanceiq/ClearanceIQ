FDA prior notice checklist for food shipments

Prior notice is required before arrival. Use this checklist to avoid the most common submission gaps.

1. Submit before arrival

Confirm the submission timing rule by mode: air, rail, truck, or vessel. Late submissions can trigger refusal or hold.

2. Confirm manufacturer, shipper, and consignee fields

Name, address, and contact should be consistent across the bill of lading and FDA prior notice.

3. Harmonized product details first

Prior notice asks for article/commodity, manufacturer info, and growing location. Have HTS and product details ready.

4. Save confirmation numbers

Keep the FDA confirmation number with your shipment docs. If CBP or FDA asks, you want it on file.
